What can we see?

This page outlines some of the trends discovered in the creation of the dashboard

Spending Increase

From the net spend chart we can see that there has been a steady increase in net spend from Premier League clubs, since 2012 net spending has risen by half a billion Euro from just over 200 million to just over 700 million.

Transfer Value Groups

From the row chart we can see that the vast majority of transfers are below 20 million Euro.

Full transfers versus Loans

This pie chart shows that Premier League clubs rarely dip into the loan market and focus on permanent transfers instead.
