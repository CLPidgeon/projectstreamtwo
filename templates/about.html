The Football Transfer Window

Welcome to this football transfers site, all data has been compiled using transfermarkt and all data is in Euro.

What data is involved?

The data in this database spans 10 premier league seasons. The year symbolises the year in which each season ended. Two types of transfer are recorded - a full transfer and a loan with a fee, free transfers and loans which didn't involve a fee were not recorded.

What is the dashboard for?

The dashboard provides visual displays of the data from net spend to splitting the transfer value into groups. The charts are fully interactive and you can select a specific club or season to look for specific data.There are also simple number displays for the number of transfers completed, the total net spend and the highest value transfer.

What does each chart show?

To discover what each chart is showing click the 'Chart Tour' button - this will go through each chart and give an explanation as to what each chart is displaying.
