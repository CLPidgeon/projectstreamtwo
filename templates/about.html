The Football Transfer Window

Welcome to this football transfers site, all data has been compiled using the transfermarkt website and all transfer data is in Euro.

What data is involved?

The data in this database spans 10 premier league seasons. The year symbolises the year in which each season ended for example, 2011 encompasses the 2010-11 season. Two types of transfer are recorded; a full transfer and a loan with a fee - free transfers and loans which didn't involve a fee were not recorded. The data is only recorded if a team was in the Premier League that season, if a team has been up and down their transfer data is only recorded for the seasons they were in the league.

What is the dashboard for?

The dashboard provides visual displays of the data from net spend to splitting the transfer value into groups. The charts are fully interactive and you can select a specific club or season to look for specific data. There are also simple number displays for the number of transfers completed, the total net spend and the highest value transfer. This allows for comparisons to be made across seasons in general and allows for the tracking of an individual teams spending.

What does each chart show?

To discover what each chart is showing click the 'Chart Tour' button on the dashboard page - this will go through each chart and give an explanation as to what each chart is displaying.
